# Travel Guide: New York City, USA (NYC)

## General Overview
New York City comprises 5 boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean. At its core is Manhattan, a densely populated borough that’s among the world’s major commercial, financial, and cultural centers.

## Top Landmarks & Attractions
1. **Central Park:**
   * *Description:* The massive park in the heart of Manhattan featuring lakes, walking paths, and the Bethesda Fountain.
   * *Hours:* 06:00 AM – 01:00 AM daily.
   * *Estimated Visit Duration:* 2–4 hours.
   * *Admission:* Free.

2. **Empire State Building:**
   * *Description:* Legendary 102-story Art Deco skyscraper in Midtown Manhattan.
   * *Hours:* 08:00 AM – 11:00 PM daily.
   * *Estimated Visit Duration:* 2 hours.
   * *Admission:* Approximately $44 – $79 depending on the deck packages.

3. **Metropolitan Museum of Art (The Met):**
   * *Description:* One of the world's largest and finest art museums, located on Museum Mile.
   * *Hours:* 10:00 AM – 05:00 PM (open until 09:00 PM on Fridays and Saturdays).
   * *Estimated Visit Duration:* 3–4 hours.
   * *Admission:* $30.

## Budget & Cost-Saving Tips
* **MetroCard / OMNY:** Tap-to-ride using contactless credit cards or phones (OMNY). After 12 taps in a week, any subsequent subway or local bus ride is free.
* **TKTS Booths:** Visit the TKTS booth in Times Square to get 50% off same-day Broadway show tickets.
* **Free Museum Days:** Several museums offer pay-as-you-wish or free entry slots (e.g. Staten Island Museum, Bronx Museum of the Arts).

## Visa & Entry Documentation Requirements
* **ESTA (Electronic System for Travel Authorization):** Travelers from Visa Waiver Program countries (UK, EU, Japan, etc.) must pre-apply for ESTA.
* **B1/B2 Visitor Visa:** Other nationalities must secure a tourist visa via their local US Embassy.

## Safety & Local Advisories
* **Subway Caution:** Exercise normal safety precautions. Avoid empty cars at night and stand clear of the platform edge.
* **Street Scams:** Decline CD offers from street artists or photos with dressed-up characters in Times Square, as they demand aggressive tips.
* **Emergency Numbers:** Dial 911 for all police, fire, or medical emergencies.
